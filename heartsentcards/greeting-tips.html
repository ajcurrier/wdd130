Greeting Tips

Coming up blank? Sometimes it’s hard to get over that initial bump of knowing what to say, but don’t worry, we’re here to help! First off, you need to decide what occasion the card is for. Is it a 'just because' friendship card, a birthday card, holiday card? Below, we’ve listed some ideas to get you started:

Friendship

What is it about your friendship that is special to you?
What is something you admire about them? Give them an example of that quality.
Inside jokes? Anything special about the day you met?

Birthday

I am happy you were born so I could have such a great friend.

Holiday Cards

Hoping your holidays are filled with love, family, and happiness.

Wishing you a season that’s merry and bright

Frankincense, gold, and myrr.
